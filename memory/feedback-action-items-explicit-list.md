**How to apply:** Every status/wrap-up reply: (1) numbered action items split by owner (mine in-flight vs. his), one line each, carrying ZERO asks — pure state; (2) at most ONE decision, on its own line at the end, visually separate from the list, and it is the only ask in the turn — never woven into an item line, never re-buried as a passing mention ("say the word if…"). A finding that needs his call is not "reported" until the question is asked, but the other unanswered decisions wait for their own turns instead of stacking into this one.
